Reguler Mathematics Course (P1 ~ P3)

Details

Target: P1 ~ P3

Class Time: 1 Hour

Tuition: $850/4 Lessons

Course Introduction

~ Introduce students to think through mathematical concepts. Inspire students to discover the fun of mathematics. The characteristics of mathematical concepts and the mathematical relationship between things.

~ Let students understand the practicability in daily life through daily application questions.

~ Improve students' thinking ability and calculation accuracy through teaching skillful calculations.

~ Ensure the independent progress of each student through mock test papers.

~ Each class is supplemented in small groups to ensure that each student's independent progress can be taken care of.
